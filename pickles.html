Pickles


Definitely not an obvious thing,
you put in fresh cucumbers with salt, spices in a jar and ...
after 4 days they are... Pickles

You can make pickles from any vegetable or fruit, even a watermelon ...
It is hard to believe but there are many other foods that can be preserved.
The origin of the custom is the need to preserve food for periods when food is not abundant.
In all cultures the custom does not always exist with the same ingredients or with the same methods,
but... you have canned food for a time of scarcity.
