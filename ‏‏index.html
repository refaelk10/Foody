Introduction

Hello, my name is Refael and I LOVE cooking, make pickles and spreads, pesto and such.
As a small child I joined my mother mainly in making cakes, at a later stage I took it upon myself to prepare food,
the experience I gained over the years.
I usually do not cook according to a recipe, these are used to fill in missing information,
On this site I will share with you my love for the kitchen
